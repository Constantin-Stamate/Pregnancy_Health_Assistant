About the App

Pregnancy Health Assistant

Our app, **Pregnancy Health Assistant**, is built using a machine learning model. After analyzing and comparing three models - **Gradient Boosting**, **Logistic Regression** and **Decision Tree** - we chose to use **Gradient Boosting** due to its superior performance. It analyzes medical data and predicts the health status of the fetus. The model has an accuracy of **97.4%**, reliably predicting the health status of the fetus.
